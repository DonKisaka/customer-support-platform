SmartFlow CRM - Product Overview

SmartFlow CRM is a cloud-based customer relationship management platform designed to help businesses manage customer interactions, track sales pipelines, and automate support workflows.

Key Features:
- Contact Management: Store and manage all customer and prospect information in one place.
- Sales Pipeline: Visualize and track deals through customizable pipeline stages.
- Email Integration: Sync with Gmail and Outlook to log all customer communications automatically.
- Task Automation: Create workflows that trigger actions based on customer behavior or deal stage changes.
- Reporting Dashboard: Generate real-time reports on sales performance, customer activity, and team productivity.
- Customer Support Ticketing: Built-in ticketing system to manage and resolve customer issues efficiently.

Editions:
- Starter: Up to 5 users, basic CRM features, email integration.
- Professional: Up to 25 users, sales automation, reporting, and API access.
- Enterprise: Unlimited users, advanced automation, custom integrations, dedicated support.

SmartFlow CRM integrates with over 200 third-party tools including Slack, Zapier, Shopify, QuickBooks, and Stripe.
